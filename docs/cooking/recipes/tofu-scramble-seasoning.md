# Tofu Scramble Seasoning

> Total: 10 mins · Yield: about ½ cup · Cost: under $3

## Ingredients

### For the seasoning

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | nutritional yeast |
| 4 teaspoons | black salt (also called kala namak) |
| 3 teaspoons | onion powder |
| 1 teaspoon | garlic powder |
| 1 teaspoon | turmeric |

### For the tofu scramble (serves 4)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 tablespoon | vegan butter or oil |
| 1 block (12–15 ounces) | medium-firm, firm, or extra-firm tofu |
| 2 tablespoons | Tofu Scramble Seasoning |

## Preparation

### To make the seasoning

1. Combine all seasoning ingredients in a sealable jar; stir or shake until evenly mixed. Store in the pantry for up to 2 years.

### To make the tofu scramble

1. Heat the vegan butter or oil in a pan. Crumble the tofu into the hot pan, sprinkle with 2 tablespoons seasoning, and stir to coat. Cook about 5 minutes, until heated through.

## Notes

- Tofu firmness: medium-firm yields a creamy, tender scramble; firm is slightly less tender; extra-firm gives a drier scramble.
- Make ahead: prepared scramble keeps in an airtight container in the fridge for about 3 days. Eat cold or gently reheat in a pan or microwave.
