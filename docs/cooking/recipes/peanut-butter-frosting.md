# Peanut Butter Frosting

> Total: 15 mins · Yield: about 3½ cups (enough for one 2-layer cake or 24 cupcakes) · Cost: under $5

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | peanut butter (use sweetened peanut butter, not the natural kind, which can separate) |
| 3 cups | powdered sugar |
| 1 teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

## Preparation

1. Add the butter and peanut butter to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar. Mix in the vanilla, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

## Notes

- Quick tip: If using to frost the [Yellow Cake Mix](cooking/recipes/yellow-cake-mix.md) or [Devil's Food Cake Mix](cooking/recipes/devils-food-cake-mix.md), make sure to let your cake or cupcakes cool completely beforehand.
