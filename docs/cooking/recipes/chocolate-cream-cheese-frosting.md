# Chocolate Cream Cheese Frosting

> Total: 15 mins · Yield: about 3½ cups (enough for one 2-layer cake or 24 cupcakes) · Cost: under $5

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 2¾ cups | powdered sugar |
| ¼ cup | cocoa powder |
| 1 tablespoon | apple cider vinegar |
| 2 teaspoons | vanilla extract |
| 1 teaspoon | lemon juice |
| 1–3 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

## Preparation

1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar and cocoa powder. Mix in the vinegar, vanilla, and lemon juice, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

## Notes

- Quick tip: If using to frost the [Yellow Cake Mix](cooking/recipes/yellow-cake-mix.md) or [Devil's Food Cake Mix](cooking/recipes/devils-food-cake-mix.md), make sure to let your cake or cupcakes cool completely beforehand.
- Quick tip: Vegetable shortening helps the frosting hold up well at room temperature, but you can use all vegan butter if you prefer. Keep everything cold; you may need less or no plant-based milk as the frosting will likely be softer.
