# Tacos & More Seasoning

> Total: 5 mins · Yield: about ¼ cup · Cost: under $2

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 2 tablespoons | chili powder |
| 1 tablespoon | ground cumin |
| 1 tablespoon | onion powder |
| 1 tablespoon | garlic powder |
| 1 tablespoon | paprika (use smoked paprika for extra yum) |
| 1 teaspoon | oregano |
| ¼ teaspoon | cayenne |

## Preparation

1. Add the spices to a jar and shake to combine. Stays fresh for 1–2 years.
