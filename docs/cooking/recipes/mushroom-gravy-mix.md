# Mushroom Gravy Mix

> Total: 15 mins · Yield: about 1 cup mix (5 batches gravy) · Cost: under $5 · Equipment: spice/coffee grinder

## Ingredients

### For the mix

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ¼ ounce | dried mushrooms (2½ tablespoons powdered) |
| ¾ cup | all-purpose flour |
| 1 tablespoon | onion powder |
| 1 tablespoon | garlic powder |
| 1 teaspoon | ground sage |
| 1 teaspoon | dried thyme leaves |
| ¼ teaspoon | black pepper |

### For the gravy

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1–1½ cups | cold water |
| 3 tablespoons | Mushroom Gravy Mix |
| 2–4 teaspoons | soy sauce |

## Preparation

### To make the mix

1. Grind the dried mushrooms to a powder in a coffee or spice grinder.
2. Combine the mushroom powder, flour, onion powder, garlic powder, sage, thyme, and pepper in a sealable jar; stir or shake to distribute evenly. Store in the pantry for up to 6 months.

### To make the gravy

1. In a small saucepan, whisk cold water, 3 tablespoons mix, and 2 teaspoons soy sauce. (Use 1 cup water for thicker gravy, 1½ for thinner.) Set over medium heat, whisking often, until thickened, 2–5 minutes. Adjust soy sauce to taste.

## Notes

- Make ahead: store prepared gravy airtight in the fridge for up to 3 days or in the freezer for up to 2 months. Reheat gently on the stove or in the microwave; whisk in a splash of water to thin if needed. Thaw before reheating if frozen.
