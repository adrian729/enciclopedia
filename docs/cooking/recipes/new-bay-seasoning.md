# New Bay Seasoning

> Total: 5 mins · Yield: about ¼ cup · Cost: under $2

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 tablespoon | ground bay leaves (see Notes) |
| 1 tablespoon | celery salt (see Notes) |
| 2 teaspoons | paprika |
| 1 teaspoon | black pepper |
| 1 teaspoon | dry mustard |
| ¼ teaspoon | cayenne |
| ⅛ teaspoon | ground nutmeg |
| ⅛ teaspoon | cinnamon |
| ⅛ teaspoon | ground cloves |

## Preparation

1. Add the spices to a jar and shake to combine. Stays fresh for 1–2 years.

## Notes

- If your store doesn't sell ground bay leaves, grind whole dried bay leaves to a powder in a spice or coffee grinder. Ground sage works as a substitute for a different but similar seasoning.
- Substitute 1 tablespoon celery salt with 1½ teaspoons celery seed plus 1½ teaspoons salt.
