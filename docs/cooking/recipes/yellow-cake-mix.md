# Yellow Cake Mix

> Total: 30 mins · Yield: about 8 cups (4 one-layer cakes or 48 cupcakes) · Cost: under $3

## Ingredients

### For the mix

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 5 cups | all-purpose flour |
| 3 cups | white sugar |
| 4 teaspoons | baking soda |
| 2 teaspoons | salt |
| 1 teaspoon | turmeric (for color) |

### For the one-layer cake or 12 cupcakes

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 2 cups | Yellow Cake Mix |
| ⅔ cup | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| ⅓ cup | light oil, such as canola or vegetable |
| 2 tablespoons | lemon juice |
| 2 teaspoons | vanilla extract |

### For the mug cake (serves 1)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ¼ cup | Yellow Cake Mix |
| 2 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| 1 tablespoon | light oil, such as canola or vegetable |
| 1 teaspoon | vegan-friendly sprinkles (optional) |
| ¼ teaspoon | vanilla extract |

## Preparation

### To make the mix

1. Combine flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and turmeric in a large jar or sealable container; whisk to distribute evenly. Store in the pantry for up to 1 year.

### To make the one-layer cake or 12 cupcakes

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a 9-inch round cake pan and line the bottom with parchment, or line a 12-count cupcake pan with liners.
2. In a large bowl, mix 2 cups cake mix, plant-based milk, oil, lemon juice, and vanilla. Don't overmix; lumps are fine.
3. **Cake:** Spread the batter into the prepared pan and bake 22–28 minutes, until lightly golden and a toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely before unmolding.
4. **Cupcakes:** Divide evenly among the 12 wells and bake 18–22 minutes, until lightly golden on top and a toothpick comes out clean. Cool in the pan.

### To make the mug cake

1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix ¼ cup cake mix, plant-based milk, oil, sprinkles (if using), and vanilla.
2. Microwave on high about 1 minute 30 seconds, until risen but slightly damp on top. Cool 5 minutes before eating.

## Notes

- Make ahead: freeze baked, cooled, unfrosted cakes or cupcakes. Thaw at room temperature (about 3 hours) and enjoy plain or with thawed frosting.
