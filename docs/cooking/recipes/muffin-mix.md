# Muffin Mix

> Total: 25 mins · Yield: 5⅓ cups (4 batches muffins) · Cost: under $3

## Ingredients

### For the mix

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 3½ cups | all-purpose flour |
| 1½ cups | brown sugar or white sugar |
| 2 teaspoons | baking powder |
| 2 teaspoons | baking soda |
| 1 teaspoon | salt |

### For the muffins (makes 6)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1⅓ cups | Muffin Mix |
| ½ cup | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| ¼ cup | apple sauce or mashed banana |
| ¼ cup | vegan butter, softened (not melted) |

### Add-ins (choose 1–3, optional)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| ½ teaspoon | almond extract |
| 1 teaspoon | cinnamon or [Pumpkin Pie Spice](cooking/recipes/pumpkin-pie-spice.md) (or store-bought) |
| 1–2 tablespoons | lemon, lime, orange, or grapefruit zest |
| ½ cup | fresh or frozen berries |
| ½ cup | vegan chocolate chips or chopped dark chocolate |
| ½ cup | chopped apples, bananas, peaches, or other fruit |
| ¼ cup | vegan-friendly sprinkles |
| ¼ cup | chopped almonds, walnuts, pecans, pistachios, or hazelnuts |
| ¼ cup | shredded or flaked coconut |
| 2 tablespoons | poppy or sesame seeds |

## Preparation

### To make the mix

1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large jar; shake or stir to distribute evenly. Store in the pantry for up to 1 year.

### To make the muffins

1. Preheat the oven to 375°F. Lightly grease a six-count muffin pan or line with paper cups.
2. In a large bowl, stir together 1⅓ cups muffin mix, plant-based milk, apple sauce or banana, and softened vegan butter. Stir in any extracts, spices, or zest now. Mix just until combined; lumps are fine (overmixing prevents a proper rise). Fold in any fruit, chocolate, nuts, coconut, or seeds.
3. Divide evenly among the six wells and bake 13–18 minutes, until golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

## Notes

- Flavor combos worth trying: lemon and poppy seed; almond extract with sprinkles (birthday-cake style); blueberries with lime zest; pumpkin pie spice with coconut.
- Make ahead: cool completely, then freeze airtight for up to 3 months. Thaw at room temperature.
