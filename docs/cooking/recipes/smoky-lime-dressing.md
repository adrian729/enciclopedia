# Smoky Lime Dressing

> Total: 5 mins · Yield: about ⅓ cup · Cost: under $3

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 3 tablespoons | vegan mayonnaise |
| 3 tablespoons | lime juice |
| 1 clove | garlic, minced or pressed |
| ½ teaspoon | smoked paprika |
| ¼ teaspoon | salt |

## Preparation

1. Add all the ingredients to a sealable jar and shake well to combine.

## Notes

- Storage: keeps fresh for up to 5 days when kept in an airtight container in the fridge.
