# Apple Chickpea Salad

> Total: 15 mins · Yield: 2 servings as a main or 4 as a side · Cost: under $7

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 can (19 ounces) | chickpeas, drained and rinsed (about 2 cups) |
| 1 | apple (any kind), chopped |
| 2 stalks | celery, thinly sliced |
| ¼ | red onion, chopped |
| ¼ cup | raisins |
| 1 batch | [Maple Dijon Dressing](cooking/recipes/maple-dijon-dressing.md) |

## Preparation

1. Add the chickpeas, apples, celery, onions, and raisins to a large bowl. Toss to combine, drizzle with the dressing to taste, and then toss again to coat.

## Notes

- Storage: stores well covered in the fridge for 4–5 days.
