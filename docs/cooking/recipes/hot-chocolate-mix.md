# Hot Chocolate Mix

> Total: 5 mins · Yield: 2 cups mix, 5 servings · Cost: under $3

## Ingredients

### For the Hot Chocolate Mix

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 cup | cocoa powder |
| 1 cup | white sugar |
| ½ teaspoon | salt |

### For the hot chocolate (serves 1)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 3 tablespoons | Hot Chocolate Mix |
| 1 cup | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| A dollop of | coconut cream (optional, for serving) |

## Preparation

### To make the mix

1. Combine cocoa powder, sugar, and salt in an airtight container; stir or shake to distribute evenly. Store in the pantry for up to 2 years.

### To make the hot chocolate

1. **Stovetop:** In a small saucepan over medium heat, whisk 3 tablespoons mix into the plant-based milk. Heat about 3 minutes, until hot and well combined. Top with coconut cream if desired.
2. **Microwave:** In a large mug, stir 3 tablespoons mix into the plant-based milk (leave headroom for expansion). Heat in 30-second bursts, stirring between each, until hot and smooth. Top with coconut cream if desired.

## Notes

- Make ahead: prepare a day in advance, cool completely, and refrigerate. Reheat gently on the stove or in the microwave, whisking as needed.
