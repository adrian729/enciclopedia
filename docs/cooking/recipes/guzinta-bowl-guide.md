## Preparation

1. For your bowl to be a success, it's important you stick within a theme so that you don't end up with weird combinations of ingredients. So first assess your fridge and the ingredients you have on hand, then decide on a theme—Mexican, Thai, barbecue, Italian, Mediterranean, etc.
2. Now pick your base and put a large scoop in the bottom of your bowl. You can choose to enjoy the base hot or cold. Follow this with your hearty selections, leftovers, veggies, flavor boosters, and sauces. Make sure you follow your theme as you go so that you end up with a bowl of flavors that complement each other. Then enjoy!

## Notes

- Quick tip: cook up extra grains whenever a recipe calls for them so that you can make a Guzinta Bowl later in the week.
- Quick tip: if your beans or tofu are unseasoned, you can easily jazz them up by heating them up in a small saucepan with 1–4 tablespoons barbecue sauce. Or heat 1 teaspoon oil with 1 teaspoon spice mix, then toss with the tofu or beans.
- Quick tip: you can quickly cook a potato in the microwave by first piercing it all over with a fork to release steam, then microwaving it on high for 5–10 minutes, depending on the size of the potato, until tender all the way through.
- Mexican combo: white rice, black beans, smoked tofu, romaine lettuce, corn, salsa, and [Avocado Cilantro Sauce](cooking/recipes/avocado-cilantro-sauce.md).
- Thai combo: rice noodles, medium-firm tofu tossed in a splash of soy sauce, edamame, grated carrots, red cabbage, peanuts, green onions, sesame seeds, [Peanut Lime Dressing](cooking/recipes/peanut-lime-dressing.md), and Sriracha.
- BBQ combo: quinoa, kidney beans simmered in barbecue sauce, cooked sweet potato, cucumbers, sliced bell peppers, caramelized onions, and pepitas.
- Italian combo: quinoa, lima beans heated with [Italian Seasoning](cooking/recipes/italian-seasoning.md), arugula, sautéed mushrooms, red onions, grape tomatoes, parsley, and balsamic glaze.
- Mediterranean combo: couscous, chickpeas heated with olive oil and garlic powder, mixed greens, sliced sun-dried tomatoes, chopped walnuts, olives, chopped parsley, lemon juice, and olive oil.
