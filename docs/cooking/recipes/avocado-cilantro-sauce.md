# Avocado Cilantro Sauce

> Total: 5 mins · Yield: about 1 cup · Cost: under $5 · Equipment: blender or food processor

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 small | avocado, peeled and pitted |
| ½ cup | fresh cilantro |
| ¼ cup | water |
| 1 clove | garlic |
| 1 tablespoon | lime juice |
| 1 teaspoon | agave or corn syrup |
| ¼ teaspoon | salt |

## Preparation

1. Add all the ingredients to a food processor or blender, and blend until your sauce is smooth.

## Notes

- Best used fresh.
