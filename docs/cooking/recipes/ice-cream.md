## Preparation

### Basic Ice Cream

1. Add the bananas to a food processor or high-powered blender and blend, stopping to scrape the sides as needed, until you reach a smooth consistency. If the ice cream is too thick, add 1 tablespoon at a time of plant-based milk until you reach a completely smooth soft-serve texture. The less plant-based milk you add, the firmer the ice cream will be. You can firm up the ice cream even more by popping it in the freezer for about 20–30 minutes. This ice cream is best enjoyed within a few hours of preparation.
2. If you plan to make any of the additional flavor options, follow the directions below. Read those instructions first, as there may be a slight variation to this basic recipe.

### Vanilla

1. Blend the vanilla and corn syrup along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Chocolate

1. Blend the cocoa powder and corn syrup along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Double Chocolate

1. Blend the cocoa powder and corn syrup along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Add chocolate chips and pulse a few times to mix in.

### Peanut Butter

1. Blend the peanut butter along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Chocolate Peanut Butter

1. Blend the cocoa powder and corn syrup along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Drizzle in the peanut butter and pulse to swirl.

### Strawberry Swirl

1. Blend the strawberries and vanilla along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Add the strawberry jam and pulse to swirl.

### Mint Chocolate Chip

1. Blend the peppermint extract and food coloring along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Add the chocolate chips and pulse a few times to mix in.

### Cookies 'N' Cream

1. Once the Basic Ice Cream ingredients are blended into a smooth ice cream, add the cookies and pulse a few times to mix in.

### Coffee

1. Blend the espresso powder and corn syrup along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Mocha

1. Blend the cocoa powder, corn syrup, and espresso powder along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

1. Blend the vanilla along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Add the cookie dough and chocolate chips and pulse a few times to mix in.

### Butter Pecan

1. Blend the vegan butter, maple syrup, and vanilla along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Add the pecans and pulse a few times to mix in.

### Cherry Chip

1. Blend the cherries, corn syrup, and vanilla along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Add the chocolate chips and pulse a few times to mix in.

### Pistachio

1. Blend ¼ cup pistachios and food coloring, if desired, along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons pistachios and pulse a few times to mix in, for texture.

### Piña Colada

1. Blend the coconut milk and pineapple along with the bananas, until smooth. Add the shredded coconut and pulse a few times to mix in.

### Pumpkin Spice

1. Blend the pumpkin purée, maple syrup, and pumpkin pie spice along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Mixed Berry

1. Blend the berries and corn syrup along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Mango

1. Blend the mangoes and corn syrup along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.

### Lemon

1. Blend the lemon juice, corn syrup, and lemon zest along with the Basic Ice Cream ingredients, until smooth.
