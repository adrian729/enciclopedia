# Pancake & Waffle Mix

> Total: 15 mins · Yield: about 4½ cups (3 batches) · Cost: under $3

## Ingredients

### For the mix

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 4 cups | all-purpose flour |
| ⅓ cup | white sugar |
| 3 tablespoons | baking powder |
| 1 teaspoon | salt |

### For the pancakes or waffles (makes 6–8 pancakes or 3–4 waffles)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1½ cups | Pancake & Waffle Mix |
| 1¼ cups | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| 2½ tablespoons | light oil, (such as canola or vegetable), plus more for frying |
| 1 tablespoon | lemon juice or apple cider vinegar |

### Add-ins (optional)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| ½ teaspoon | cinnamon or [Pumpkin Pie Spice](cooking/recipes/pumpkin-pie-spice.md) (or store-bought) |
| ½ cup | berries, sliced bananas, chopped apples, or vegan chocolate chips |

## Preparation

### To make the mix

1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large jar or sealable bag. Whisk or shake to distribute evenly. Store airtight at room temperature for up to 1 year.

### To make the pancakes or waffles

1. In a medium bowl, stir together 1½ cups mix, plant-based milk, oil, and lemon juice. Add any extracts, spices, or zest now. Mix just until combined; lumps are fine. Gently fold in fruit or chocolate chips if using.
2. **Pancakes:** Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat. Scoop ⅓-cup portions of batter and gently spread. Cook until large bubbles form and the bottom is golden, then flip and cook 1–2 minutes more until golden.
3. **Waffles:** Heat the waffle iron and mist with spray oil. Spoon about ⅔ cup batter onto the iron and close. Cook until golden on both sides, then lift out with a fork.

## Notes

- To keep cooked pancakes or waffles warm, hold them in a heatproof dish in a 150°F–200°F oven.
- Make ahead: cool completely, freeze in a single layer until solid (about 2 hours), then transfer to an airtight container or bag and freeze for up to 2 months. Reheat in a toaster.
