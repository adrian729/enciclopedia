# Mac & Cheese Powder

> Total: 15 mins · Yield: about 1 cup (4 batches mac & cheese) · Cost: under $5

## Ingredients

### For the powder

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ¾ cup | nutritional yeast |
| ½ cup | all-purpose flour (sub whole wheat or all-purpose gluten-free flour if preferred) |
| 1 tablespoon | white sugar |
| 1 tablespoon | paprika |
| 2 teaspoons | salt |
| 2 teaspoons | onion powder |
| 1 teaspoon | garlic powder |
| 1 teaspoon | lemon pepper |
| ½ teaspoon | turmeric |

### For the mac & cheese, stovetop method (serves 2)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 cup | uncooked macaroni noodles (gluten-free if preferred) |
| ¾ cup | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| ¼ cup | Mac & Cheese Powder |

### For the mac & cheese, microwave method (serves 1)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | uncooked macaroni noodles (gluten-free if preferred) |
| ¾ cup | water |
| 3 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| 2 tablespoons | Mac & Cheese Powder |

### For the cheese sauce (use as a dip or with other dishes)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ¾ cup | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| ¼ cup | Mac & Cheese Powder |

## Preparation

### To make the powder

1. Combine nutritional yeast, flour, sugar, paprika, salt, onion powder, garlic powder, lemon pepper, and turmeric in an airtight container; stir or shake to distribute evenly. Store in the pantry for up to 1 year.

### To make the stovetop mac & cheese

1. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil and cook the macaroni per package directions. Drain, rinse, and return the empty pot to the (turned-off) stove.
2. Whisk plant-based milk and ¼ cup mac & cheese powder into the pot. Set heat to medium-high and whisk until thickened, 2–5 minutes.
3. Stir in the cooked pasta to coat.

### To make the microwave mac & cheese

1. In a large microwave-safe mug or bowl, combine macaroni and water. Microwave on high for 2–5 minutes until tender; drain off excess water.
2. Stir in plant-based milk and 2 tablespoons mac & cheese powder; microwave 30 seconds more.

### To make the cheese sauce

1. Whisk plant-based milk and ¼ cup mac & cheese powder in a small pot over medium-high heat until thickened, 2–5 minutes. Microwave alternative: whisk in a microwave-safe bowl, heating in 30-second bursts and stirring between, until thickened, 2–3 minutes.
