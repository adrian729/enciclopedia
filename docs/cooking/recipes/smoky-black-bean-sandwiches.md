# Smoky Black Bean Sandwiches

> Total: 10 mins · Yield: 4 servings · Cost: under $7

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 can (19 ounces) | black beans, drained and rinsed (about 2 cups) |
| 2 stalks | celery, chopped |
| ¼ | red onion, finely chopped |
| 2 tablespoons | vegan mayonnaise |
| ½–1 | jalapeño, minced (optional) |
| ½ teaspoon | smoked paprika |
| ¼ teaspoon | salt |
| 8 slices | bread, optionally toasted (gluten-free if preferred) |
| 4 | lettuce leaves |

## Preparation

1. Add the black beans to a medium bowl and use a potato masher or fork to smash the beans until about half of the beans are mashed.
2. Add the celery, onions, mayonnaise, jalapeños (if using), paprika, and salt and stir to combine.
3. Make the sandwiches by dividing the filling evenly among four slices of bread. Top with lettuce and the remaining slices of bread and enjoy!
