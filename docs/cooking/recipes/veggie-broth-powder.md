# Veggie Broth Powder

> Total: 10 mins · Yield: about ¼ cup powder (12 cups broth) · Cost: under $1

## Ingredients

### For the powder

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 2 teaspoons | dried parsley |
| 2 teaspoons | dried thyme leaves |
| 2 teaspoons | onion powder |
| 2 teaspoons | garlic powder |
| 2 teaspoons | salt |
| 1 teaspoon | dried basil |
| 1 teaspoon | celery seed |
| ¼ teaspoon | black pepper |
| ½ teaspoon | turmeric |

### For the broth

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 teaspoon | Veggie Broth Powder |
| 1 cup | hot water |

## Preparation

### To make the powder

1. Combine all powder ingredients in an airtight container; stir or shake to distribute evenly. Store in the pantry for up to 3 years.

### To make the broth

1. Stir 1 teaspoon broth powder into 1 cup hot water. The broth carries flecks of spices; for a smooth result, steep 5–10 minutes and strain through a fine-mesh strainer.

## Notes

- Make ahead: prepare the broth, cool, and store airtight in the fridge for 4–5 days. Stir or shake before use.
