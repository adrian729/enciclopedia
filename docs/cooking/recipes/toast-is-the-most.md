> Total: 10 mins · Yield: 1 serving · Cost: $1–$3

## Preparation

1. Toast your bread of choice.
2. Add a spread, one to two toppings, and any desired flavor boosters. Enjoy.

## Notes

- Combo idea — Avocado Arugula: avocado, arugula, red onion, and a sprinkle of Cajun spice mix.
- Combo idea — Pear Walnut: vegan cream cheese, slices of pear, walnuts, maple syrup, and cinnamon.
- Combo idea — Hummus Cucumber: hummus, cucumber, balsamic glaze, and black pepper.
- Combo idea — BBQ Lentil: lentils mixed with barbecue sauce, tomato slices, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper.
- Combo idea — Banana Raisin: peanut butter, banana, raisins, and shredded coconut.
- Combo idea — Radish Cilantro: [Hummamole](cooking/recipes/hummamole.md), radish slices, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
- Combo idea — Cream Cheese Cantaloupe: vegan cream cheese, cantaloupe, sliced almonds, and agave.
- Combo idea — Lemon Raspberry: vegan cream cheese, raspberries, lemon zest, and poppy seeds.
- Combo idea — PB&S: peanut butter, strawberry slices, and lime zest.
