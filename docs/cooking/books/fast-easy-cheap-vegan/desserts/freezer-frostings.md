# Freezer Frostings

> Makes: About 3½ cups (enough for one 2-layer cake or 24 cupcakes) · Total time: 15 minutes · Total cost: Under $5

## Ingredients

### Vanilla
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 3 cups | powdered sugar |
| 1 teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

### Chocolate
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 2¾ cups | powdered sugar |
| ¼ cup | cocoa powder |
| 1 teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

### Peppermint
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 3 cups | powdered sugar |
| 2 teaspoons | peppermint extract |
| 1 teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |
| A few drops | vegan green food coloring (optional) |

### Gingerbread
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 3 cups | powdered sugar |
| ¼ cup | molasses |
| 1 teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1 teaspoon | [Pumpkin Pie Spice](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/mixes/diy-spice-mixes.md?id=pumpkin-pie-spice) (or store-bought) |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

### Cinnamon Cream Cheese
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 3 cups | powdered sugar |
| 1 tablespoon | apple cider vinegar |
| 2 teaspoons | vanilla extract |
| 1 teaspoon | lemon juice |
| ½ teaspoon | cinnamon |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

### Chocolate Cream Cheese
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 2¾ cups | powdered sugar |
| ¼ cup | cocoa powder |
| 1 tablespoon | apple cider vinegar |
| 2 teaspoons | vanilla extract |
| 1 teaspoon | lemon juice |
| 1–3 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

### Peanut Butter
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | peanut butter (use sweetened peanut butter, not the natural kind, which can separate) |
| 3 cups | powdered sugar |
| 1 teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

### Strawberry
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ½ cup | vegan butter |
| ½ cup | vegetable shortening |
| 3 cups | powdered sugar |
| ¼ cup | strawberry jam (or sub another flavor of preserve) |
| 1 teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1–4 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat, if needed |

## Preparation

### Vanilla
1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar. Mix in the vanilla, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

### Chocolate
1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar and cocoa powder. Mix in the vanilla, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

### Peppermint
1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar. Mix in the peppermint and vanilla, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency. Add a few drops of green food coloring, if desired.

### Gingerbread
1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar. Mix in the molasses, vanilla, and pumpkin pie spice, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

### Cinnamon Cream Cheese
1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar. Mix in the vinegar, vanilla, lemon juice, and cinnamon, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

### Chocolate Cream Cheese
1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar and cocoa powder. Mix in the vinegar, vanilla, and lemon juice, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

### Peanut Butter
1. Add the butter and peanut butter to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar. Mix in the vanilla, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

### Strawberry
1. Add the butter and shortening to a large bowl and use a mixer or whisk to whip until fluffy. Slowly incorporate the powdered sugar. Mix in the jam and vanilla, then add 1 tablespoon of plant-based milk at a time as needed to reach desired frosting consistency.

**QUICK TIP:**
1. If using any of these frostings to frost the [Yellow Cake](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/mixes/yellow-cake-mix.md) or [Devil's Food Cake](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/mixes/devil-s-food-cake-mix.md) make sure to let your cake or cupcakes cool completely beforehand.
2. I like using vegetable shortening because it helps the frosting hold up well at room temperature, but if you prefer you can use all vegan butter. Make sure to keep everything cold, and you may need less or no plant-based milk as the frosting will likely be softer.
