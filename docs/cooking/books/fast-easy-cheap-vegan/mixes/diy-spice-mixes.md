# DIY SPICE MIXES
> MAKES | About ¼ cup (each spice mix) · TOTAL TIME | 5 minutes · TOTAL COST | Under $2 (each spice mix)

## Ingredients

### ITALIAN SEASONING
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1 tablespoon | dried basil |
| 1½ teaspoons | dried thyme leaves |
| 1½ teaspoons | dried rosemary |
| 1½ teaspoons | dried parsley |
| 1½ teaspoons | dried oregano |
| 1½ teaspoons | onion powder |
| 1½ teaspoons | garlic powder |

### TACOS & MORE SEASONING
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 2 tablespoons | chili powder |
| 1 tablespoon | ground cumin |
| 1 tablespoon | onion powder |
| 1 tablespoon | garlic powder |
| 1 tablespoon | paprika (use smoked paprika for extra yum) |
| 1 teaspoon | oregano |
| ¼ teaspoon | cayenne |

### GREEK SEASONING
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 1½ tablespoons | dried oregano |
| 1 tablespoon | dried basil |
| 1½ teaspoons | dried dill |
| 1½ teaspoons | onion powder |

| 1 teaspoon | salt |
| ½ teaspoon | black pepper |

### NEW BAY SEASONING
| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|

|---|---|

## Preparation

1. For all of these recipes, simply add the spices to a jar and shake to combine. Each recipe will stay fresh for 1–2 years.

### QUICK TIPS
1. If your store doesn’t sell ground bay leaves, you can make your own from dried bay leaves. Use a spice grinder or coffee grinder to grind bay leaves into a powder. Or you can substitute the ground bay with ground sage for a different but similar seasoning.
2. You can substitute the 1 tablespoon of celery salt with 1½ teaspoons celery seed + 1½ teaspoons salt.
