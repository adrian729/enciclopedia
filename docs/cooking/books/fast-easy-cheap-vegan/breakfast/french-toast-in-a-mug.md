# FRENCH TOAST IN A MUG

> SERVES: 1 · TOTAL TIME: 3 minutes · TOTAL COST: Under $1 (not including toppings)

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ¼ | medium banana |
| 3 tablespoons | plant-based milk, such as soy or oat |
| 1 teaspoon | maple syrup |
| 1 teaspoon | vegan butter (optional for flavor) |
| ¼ teaspoon | cinnamon |
| ¼ teaspoon | vanilla extract |
| 1 slice | bread, torn into pieces (about 1 cup, gluten-free if preferred) |
| - | Optional toppings: sliced fruit, berries, nuts, more maple syrup, chocolate chips, powdered sugar, or a drizzle of nut butter |

## Preparation

1. Add the banana to a microwave-safe mug and mash with a fork. Add the plant-based milk, maple syrup, butter, cinnamon, and vanilla and mix. It’s OK if it’s lumpy. Add the bread pieces and toss to coat.
2. Microwave for about 60 seconds, until the mixture is hot and the bread has absorbed the liquid. Enjoy hot with your favorite toppings.
