# QUICK STOVETOP GRANOLA

> MAKES | About 4 cups granola, 8 servings · TOTAL TIME | 10 minutes · TOTAL COST | $3–$7 (depending on your choices)

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
| --- | --- |
| 2 tablespoons | vegan butter or coconut oil |
| 2 cups | whole rolled oats (gluten-free if preferred) |
| ½ cup | sunflower seeds, pepitas, raw peanuts, flax seeds, sesame seeds, shredded coconut, poppy seeds, chopped almonds, or any other nut or seed you like, or a combination. |
| ¼ cup | corn syrup, agave, or maple syrup |
| ½ teaspoon | cinnamon or [Pumpkin Pie Spice](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/mixes/diy-spice-mixes.md?id=pumpkin-pie-spice) (or store-bought, optional) |
| ¼ teaspoon | salt |
| ½ cup | raisins, dried cranberries, or other dried fruit |

## Preparation

1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt the vegan butter. Once melted, add the oats, seeds and/or nuts, syrup, cinnamon, and salt. Stir the mixture as it toasts, until the oats turn golden and everything is evenly coated, about 5 minutes.
2. Remove from heat and add the dried fruit. Let cool before storing—it’ll crisp up as it cools.

**MAKE AHEAD:** Store in an airtight container in your pantry for up to 6 months.
