# TOAST IS THE MOST!

> SERVES | 1 · TOTAL TIME | 10 minutes · TOTAL COST | $1–$3 (depending on your choices)

## Ingredients

### BREAD (CHOOSE 1, GLUTEN-FREE IF PREFERRED)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
| --- | --- |
| 1 slice | bread |
| 1 piece | baguette |
| 1 | bagel |
| 1 | English muffin |

### SPREAD (CHOOSE 1)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
| --- | --- |
| ½ | avocado, mashed |
| 2 tablespoons | peanut butter |
| 2 tablespoons | other nut or seed butter |
| 2 tablespoons | vegan cream cheese |
| 2 tablespoons | classic hummus |
| 2 tablespoons | flavored hummus |
| 2 tablespoons | [Hummamole](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/snacks/hummamole.md) |
| ¼ cup | beans, mashed |
| ¼ cup | lentils |
| 2 tablespoons | canned refried beans (check to make sure they’re vegan) |

| --- | --- |
| ½ | banana, sliced |

| ¼ cup | sliced cucumbers |
| ¼ cup | raisins |
| 2 tablespoons | jam or jelly |
| 1 wedge | cantaloupe, sliced |
| ¼ cup | pineapple slices or pieces |

### FLAVOR BOOSTERS (CHOOSE 1–3, OPTIONAL)

| Quantity | Ingredient |
| --- | --- |
| - | Fresh basil leaves |

| - | Salt and black pepper |

## Preparation

1. Toast your bread of choice.
2. Add a spread, one to two toppings, and any desired flavor boosters. Enjoy!

### SOME OF MY FAVORITE COMBOS

* **Avocado Arugula:** Avocado, arugula, red onion, and a sprinkle of Cajun spice mix.
* **Pear Walnut:** Vegan cream cheese, slices of pear, walnuts, maple syrup, and cinnamon.
* **Hummus Cucumber:** Hummus, cucumber, balsamic glaze, and black pepper.
* **BBQ Lentil:** Lentils mixed with barbecue sauce, tomato slices, nutritional yeast, salt, and pepper.
* **Banana Raisin:** Peanut butter, banana, raisins, and shredded coconut.
* **Radish Cilantro:** [Hummamole](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/snacks/hummamole.md), radish slices, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
* **Cream Cheese Cantaloupe:** Vegan cream cheese, cantaloupe, sliced almonds, and agave.
* **Lemon Raspberry:** Vegan cream cheese, raspberries, lemon zest, and poppy seeds.
* **PB&S:** Peanut butter, strawberry slices, and lime zest.
