# QUICK KALE CAESAR

> SERVES | 2 as a main, 4 as a side
> TOTAL TIME | 20 minutes
> TOTAL COST | Under $7

## Ingredients

### FOR THE CROUTONS

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 2 tablespoons | olive oil |
| 3 slices | bread, cut into small cubes (about 2½ cups, gluten-free if preferred) |
| 1 tablespoon | [Tacos & More Seasoning](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/mixes/diy-spice-mixes.md?id=tacos-amp-more-seasoning) (or store-bought taco seasoning, see Quick Tip) |

### FOR THE SALAD

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 3 cups | destemmed and finely sliced kale |
| 3 cups | roughly chopped romaine lettuce |
| 1 batch | [Hummus Caesar Dressing](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/salads/dressings-sauces.md?id=hummus-caesar-dressing) |

## Preparation

1. To make the croutons, heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. When hot, add the bread cubes and seasoning and stir to evenly coat. Cook until the bread is toasted and golden all over, about 5 minutes. Set aside.
2. Add the kale and romaine to a large bowl and dress with the Caesar dressing, to taste. Toss very well to coat. Top with the croutons.

### Quick Tip

You can sub the Tacos & More Seasoning for equal amounts of [Italian Seasoning](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/mixes/diy-spice-mixes.md?id=italian-seasoning) or [Cajun Seasoning](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/mixes/diy-spice-mixes.md?id=cajun-seasoning) for other crouton flavor options.
