# ASAP ARTICHOKE SALAD

> SERVES | 2 as a main, 4 as a side
> TOTAL TIME | 15 minutes
> TOTAL COST | Under $10

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| 6 cups | baby spinach leaves |
| 1 can (19 ounces) | white beans, such as kidney or lima, drained and rinsed (about 2 cups) |
| 1 jar (6 ounces) | marinated artichoke hearts, chopped (use leftover liquid in Artichoke Dressing) |
| 1 large | tomato, cut into wedges |
| ¼ | red onion, thinly sliced |
| 1 batch | [Artichoke Dressing](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/salads/dressings-sauces.md?id=artichoke-dressing) |

## Preparation

1. Add the spinach, white beans, artichoke hearts, tomato wedges, and red onions to a large bowl. Drizzle the dressing overtop to taste, and lightly toss to coat.
