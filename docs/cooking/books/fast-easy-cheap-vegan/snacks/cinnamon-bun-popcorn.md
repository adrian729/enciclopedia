# Cinnamon Bun Popcorn

> Serves: 2 · Total time: 7 minutes · Total cost: Under $1

## Ingredients

| Quantity | Ingredient |
|---|---|
| ⅓ cup | popcorn kernels |
| 2 tablespoons | vegan butter |
| 2 tablespoons | brown sugar |
| 1 teaspoon | cinnamon |
| ¼ teaspoon | salt |

## Preparation

1. Pop the popcorn using the DIY Microwave Popcorn method ([DIY Microwave Popcorn](cooking/books/fast-easy-cheap-vegan/snacks/diy-microwave-popcorn.md), step 1) or with an air popper.
2. In a small saucepan or microwave-safe bowl, mix together the vegan butter, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt. Heat, stirring often, until the sugar is dissolved, about 2 minutes on the stove or 50 seconds in the microwave. Drizzle the cinnamon mixture over the popcorn, shaking or stirring to evenly coat the popcorn. Be careful, as it may be hot!
