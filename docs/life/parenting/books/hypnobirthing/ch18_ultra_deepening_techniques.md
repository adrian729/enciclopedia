## 1. Purpose of Ultra-Deepening

- **When these techniques are used** — during the latter part of the opening phase and into Birth Breathing; the goal is a totally limp, almost amnesiac state where the mother can go within to her birthing body and her baby
- **How to begin** — breathe yourself into a comfortable state of relaxation first (using Calm Breathing); once the body feels limp, proceed with the techniques below. They combine in sequence to progressively deepen the state

## 2. Glove Relaxation

- **Endorphin-glove visualisation** — imagine putting a soft, silver glove of natural endorphins onto your right hand. The fingers begin to feel larger and tingle, as though springs were at the fingertips. The glove causes the hand to go numb, as if plunged into icy slush
- **Birth companion's role** — the companion strokes the back of the mother's hand and arm while she visualises; the tingling and numbness spread from the hand up the arm until the limb feels senseless, like a piece of wood or leather. This plants a powerful anchor: the body becomes conditioned to react with calm whenever the hand and arm are stroked
- **Transferable numbness** — once the hand and arm lose sensation, the mother can visualise placing that hand on any part of the body (particularly the lower pelvic area) to transfer the numbing relaxation there. Even mothers who are normally uncomfortable being touched fall into relaxation with this technique combined with birthing prompts
- **Recommended as a primary practice element** — Mongan considers Glove Relaxation one of the best ways to plant an anchor for deep relaxation and one of the most effective directives during labour

## 3. The Depthometer

- **Inverted-thermometer visualisation** — picture a large, soft, flexible inverted thermometer inside your body with the bulb just above your forehead and the tube extending down to your toes. Inside the bulb is a clear fluid of natural relaxation
- **Counting down through 40 gradations** — as you count from 40 down to 0, visualise the fluid flowing down one number at a time, spreading into your circulatory system. At each decade mark (30, 20, 10, 0) relaxation doubles. By the lower teens you are in very deep relaxation; the final ten digits bring you to ultra-deep relaxation
- **Reaching the "control centre"** — at the bottom of the count you arrive at the control centre of your inner mind, where the Sensory Gate Control Valve (below) is located

## 4. The Sensory Gate Control Valve

- **Inner control-room imagery** — while in the ultra-deep state reached via the Depthometer, visualise a large control panel with lights and switches; in the centre is a round valve labelled "HypnoBirthing Valve"
- **How it works** — this valve controls messages sent from the brain past the sensory gate in the brain stem to all the nerves in the body. Turning the indicator switch to the "off" position (at eight o'clock) shuts off sensation throughout the body. Combined with Glove Relaxation, this imagery helps bring about a loss of sensation in selected body areas
- **Broader applications** — the valve imagery can also be directed toward maintaining healthy blood pressure, sustaining safe amniotic fluid levels, and controlling stress. As a self-test, suggest to yourself that your right foot is too numb and heavy to lift
- **Safety reminder** — always return the switch to the off position and release all self-hypnosis, restoring normal functions to the body before attempting to move about

## 5. Time Distortion

- **Compressing perceived time** — once skilled at self-relaxation, practise giving yourself the suggestion that every five minutes will seem as one minute. During labour, the birth companion escalates this: every twenty minutes will seem as five. This is included among the standard birthing prompts
- **Amnesia and deep withdrawal** — near the end of the thinning and opening phase, the mother becomes so deeply relaxed that it is difficult to talk and she loses awareness of the people around her. She goes more deeply into her birthing body and to her baby to begin their journey together
- **A natural gift, not a trick** — this loss of time sense and accompanying amnesia can occur in all birthings if the mother is willing to let go, bring herself deep enough, and turn her birthing over to her birthing instinct and her baby
